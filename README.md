## Overview

2BAC-HUB is a web-based platform designed to help Moroccan third-year high school students prepare for the national Baccalaureate exam.
Past national exams are the most valuable preparation resource. However, they are often scattered across unreliable websites, incomplete, poorly scanned, or difficult to navigate.
2BAC-HUB centralizes these materials into a structured, focused, and distraction-free environment where students can practice under realistic exam conditions.

---

## Problem

Students preparing for the national exam frequently face:

- Fragmented and unreliable exam sources  
- Missing corrections or incomplete documents  
- Poor scan quality  
- No structured way to simulate exam timing  
- Confusing or distracting interfaces  
ExamHub addresses these issues by providing a clean and centralized exam preparation hub.

---

## Features

-  Organized past national exams (PDF format)  
-  Integrated countdown timer for exam simulation  
-  Countdown to the national exam date  
-  Clear classification by major and shared subjects  
-  Fully responsive design  
-  Publicly deployed and accessible  

---

##  Current Structure

The platform includes:

- Three primary majors  
- Subjects shared across majors  
- Official syllabi  
- Curated, high-quality exam resources  

All implemented features are fully functional.  
Some documents are still being gathered and will be progressively integrated.

As a student preparing for the national exam myself, I experienced firsthand the difficulty of finding structured and reliable resources.

2BAC-HUB reflects my interest in using technology to solve practical educational problems and improve accessibility for other students.
